The Problem

Locked hands.
Critical information
out of reach.

During robotic surgery, the surgeon's hands are locked on instrument controls inside a sterile field. They cannot type, click, or interact with any computer system.

Critical patient data, imaging, and anatomical context require them to break scrub or rely on circulating staff — both slow, disruptive, and potentially dangerous at the wrong moment.

Sterile Field Constraint
Hands never leave the controls during critical phases
Information Latency
Relying on staff to retrieve data adds cognitive load and delay
Context Switching Cost
Breaking scrub for data interrupts surgical flow and focus
How It Works

Speak. Process. Render.

Voice Command
Surgeon speaks. 16kHz PCM audio streams via WebSocket. Wake-word filtering ignores ambient OR noise and staff conversation.
Gemini Live API
Audio and live surgical video (1 fps) processed natively. Full multimodal context — the AI understands the OR environment in real time.
Multi-Agent Routing
ADK Orchestrator routes the intent to the right specialist — patient data, CT imaging, 3D anatomy, or surgical phase context.
Visual Overlay
Results appear as glassmorphic tiles on the surgical display. ORION speaks a brief confirmation. Hands never leave the controls.
Specialist Agents

Five agents. One voice.

Each specialist is a dedicated Gemini agent with purpose-built tools and instructions. The orchestrator routes every command with zero latency.

ORION_Orchestrator
Root Agent — Wake-Word Detection & Routing

The always-on orchestrator receives all audio, filters ambient OR noise via wake-word detection, and routes each command to the correct specialist using ADK AutoFlow. Handles hide-all overlays directly. Never announces routing — just acts.

Wake-word filtering AutoFlow routing Multi-turn conversation Gemini 2.5 Flash Native Audio
IR_Agent
Information Retrieval

Retrieves and displays patient clinical data on demand — lab values, vitals, medication lists, allergies, and diagnosis — as glassmorphism tiles on the surgical display.

Labs & vitals Medications Allergies
IV_Agent
CT Image Viewer

Navigates CT scan slices by voice. Jump to anatomical landmarks — carina, aortic arch, or tumor — or scroll slice-by-slice in superior and inferior directions.

Slice navigation Landmark jump
AR_Agent
3D Anatomy Renderer

Controls a Three.js 3D lung anatomy model. Rotate to posterior, anterior, lateral, or superior views. Toggle structure visibility by name — vessels, bronchi, tumor, pleura.

3D rotation Structure toggle
PC_Agent
Procedural Context — Live Video Phase Detection

The most advanced agent. Gemini already receives the live surgical video at 1 fps via the Realtime API. PC_Agent uses that visual context to detect the current surgical phase and surface a phase-specific 4-point anatomical checklist with critical warnings — no surgeon input required for phase transitions.

Live video analysis Phase detection Anatomical checklists Critical warnings 8 surgical phases
